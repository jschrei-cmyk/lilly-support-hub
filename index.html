Getting started

How do LillyDirect's pharmacy services work?

LillyDirect is a different kind of pharmacy experience. We work with licensed third-party pharmacy service providers to give you personalized access to a convenient pharmacy experience.

We provide free home delivery for all medicines and retail pickup options for select medicines.

Where can I update my preferences and account information?

Update your account information, contact details, and preferences in your LillyDirect account settings. For support, call 1-844-LLYDIR1 (1-844-559-3471).

What makes LillyDirect different from other mail order or direct ship pharmacies?

LillyDirect's pharmacy fulfillment options provide convenient delivery options, savings guidance and resources, and support with prior authorizations.

How do I re-enable text messages from LillyDirect Pharmacy after I replied STOP?

Check your text messages. If you still have the SMS thread where you replied "STOP," you can text "START" to update. If you do not have the SMS thread, go to your LillyDirect account settings, click communication channel preferences, and select "Text" to update.

You just received my prescription. What can I expect next?

Your prescription will be reviewed to confirm required information is complete. Then, you'll receive a message with next steps. If additional information is needed, you may be contacted to update details.

How do I place an order?

Sign in to your LillyDirect account and navigate to your prescriptions page. Select the medicine you'd like to order and follow the on-screen steps to complete your order.

Can I schedule a future order?

Yes. Once your prescription is approved, you can schedule refill orders in advance through your account dashboard.

How long does delivery take?

Standard home delivery typically takes 2–5 business days after your order is processed. You'll receive tracking information once your order ships.

Which retail pharmacies support pickup?

Retail pickup is available at select pharmacy partners. Check the app or website for pharmacies near you that carry your medicine.

Paying for your Zepbound

HSA / FSA cards

Most HSA and FSA cards accepted, online or at the pharmacy counter.

Credit, debit & digital wallets

Major credit/debit cards and digital wallets accepted. For retail pickup, payment methods vary by pharmacy.
