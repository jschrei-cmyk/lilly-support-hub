How do LillyDirect's pharmacy services work?

LillyDirect is a different kind of pharmacy experience. We work with licensed third-party pharmacy service providers to give you personalized access to a convenient pharmacy experience. We provide free home delivery for all medicines and retail pickup options for select medicines.

Where can I update my preferences and account information?

Update your account information, contact details, and preferences in your LillyDirect account settings. For support, call 1-844-LLYDIR1 (1-844-559-3471).

What makes LillyDirect different from other mail order or direct ship pharmacies?

LillyDirect's pharmacy fulfillment options provide convenient delivery options, savings guidance and resources, and support with prior authorizations.

How do I re-enable text messages from LillyDirect Pharmacy after I replied STOP?

Check your text messages. If you still have the SMS thread where you replied "STOP," you can text "START" to update. If you don't have the SMS thread, go to your LillyDirect account settings, click communication channel preferences, and select "Text" to update.

You just received my prescription. What can I expect next?

Your prescription will be reviewed to confirm required information is complete. Then, you'll receive a message with next steps. If additional information is needed, you may be contacted to update details.

How do I place my first order?

After your prescriber sends a prescription to LillyDirect, you'll receive a text or email with instructions to complete your order. Follow the link to select your delivery or pickup preference and complete payment.

Can I cancel or modify an order after it's placed?

Contact LillyDirect support at 1-844-559-3471 as soon as possible. Changes may not be possible once an order has been filled or shipped.

How long does delivery take?

Most home deliveries arrive within 3–5 business days after your order is placed and your prescription is verified.

Which retail pickup locations are available?

Select Walmart locations offer retail pickup for Zepbound. After choosing pickup at checkout, LillyDirect will transfer your prescription within 1–2 business days. Walmart will then prepare your prescription within an additional 1–2 business days.

Paying for your Zepbound
